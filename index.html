The Healing Power of Hiking Alone

[12.01.25]
[Harper Woods]

There’s something incredibly freeing about hiking alone. Away from the noise of everyday life, the trail offers a space for reflection, clarity, and self-connection.

With nothing but your footsteps and the sound of nature, solo hiking can be a deeply transformative experience. In this article, we explore how hiking alone allows us to reconnect with ourselves and the natural world in ways that group hikes or busy trails cannot.

Exploring Brittany on Foot: A Journey Through Wild Coasts and Ancient Forests

[12.10.24]
[Elliot Rivers]

Brittany, with its rugged coastline and charming villages, is a paradise for hikers. Walking the region’s winding paths, you’ll encounter everything from towering cliffs to serene beaches, and ancient woodlands.

This article takes you on a journey through Brittany’s lesser-known trails, where the beauty of nature and the rich history of the land come together, inviting adventurers to discover the soul of this stunning corner of France—one step at a time.

Van Life: A Road Trip Through Europe with Sarah & Jean

[25.09.24]
[Sarah & Jean Chemi]

Join Sarah and Jean as they embark on the ultimate adventure: a solo van trip across Europe. From the rolling hills of the Scottish Highlands to the sun-drenched beaches of southern Spain, their journey is a celebration of freedom, spontaneity, and connection with the road.

In this article, Sarah and Jean share the highs and lows of van life, from finding the perfect parking spot to cooking meals under the stars, and how traveling by van changed their perspective on what it means to truly be "home."
